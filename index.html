About

Sherry Chien is a fifth-year PhD student in the Department of Linguistics at the University of California, Santa Barbara. Her research focuses on phonetics, prosody, second language acquisition (L2), and articulatory phonology. She is particularly interested in how L2 learners perceive and produce prosody and intonation, with a specific emphasis on Taiwan Mandarin English as a Foreign Language (EFL) learners. In 2024, she was awarded the Chun and Polchinski Applied Linguistics Fellowship and presented her research at the department's Spring Colloquium.
